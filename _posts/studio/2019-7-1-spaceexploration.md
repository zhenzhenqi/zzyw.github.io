**Client**: Creators Tech LLC                     
Exhibited at *CrossingArt* Gallery at Chelsea, New York

Space Exploration is an interactive, immersive installation. It creates a colorful universe filled with many types of planets and asteroids. A accompanied web application is created, allows visitors to create their own version of colorful Lightsails and launch into this universe.
